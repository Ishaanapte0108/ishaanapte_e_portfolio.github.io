Project information

  • Category: Python
  • Project date: 25 September, 2021

Engineering is the art of finding solutions to the different problems faced by the society through scientific and mathematical facilities. Engineers are solution providers to the most mammoth and challenging problems of society. As students of computer engineering this core value motivated us to take up this project. This project is our attempt at analyzing, understanding and providing an efficient and easier way to solve the complex puzzle the Rubik’s cube. In this century, everything is becoming digital so we thought of representing the solution to a Rubik’s cube in a digitalized manner. As in general, this app can simply though out a step-by-step solution that even a beginner can follow easily. The traditional way solving a Rubik’s cube comprised of analyzing the cube pieces, writing down a suitable set of rotations and performing them. Our program skips this tedious process and throws out a well framed solution. We are showing the steps of the solution on the console. The app is very convenient to use and can be used by anyone who has python installed on their computers. There are seven main steps that our app follows i.e., the white cross, white edges, middle program, yellow cross, yellow edges, aligning yellow corners and orienting yellow corners. All these steps have been explained in detail ahead. Along with this we have provided the user with options to input his scrambled cube through the on-computer camera or he can input his cube as lists representing faces of the cube in the program.
